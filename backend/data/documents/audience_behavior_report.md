# Audience Behavior Report — Q1 2025

## Executive Summary

This report analyzes viewer behavior patterns across our platform during Q1 2025. Key findings reveal shifting consumption patterns, with mobile viewing surpassing desktop for the first time, binge-watching rates increasing 18%, and significant demographic differences in content preferences.

## Viewing Patterns

### Device Preferences (Q1 2025)
- **Mobile**: 42% of total viewing time (+8% QoQ)
- **Smart TV**: 31% (-2% QoQ)
- **Desktop**: 18% (-5% QoQ)
- **Tablet**: 9% (-1% QoQ)

Mobile viewing has surpassed desktop for the first time, driven primarily by the 18-24 demographic where mobile accounts for 67% of viewing time.

### Peak Viewing Hours
- **Weekdays**: 7 PM - 11 PM (62% of daily views)
- **Weekends**: 2 PM - 12 AM (broader distribution)
- **Late Night** (12 AM - 3 AM): Growing segment, +23% YoY, primarily Horror and Thriller genres

### Binge Watching Behavior
- 34% of viewers watch 3+ episodes/movies in a single session
- Average binge session: 4.2 hours
- Most binged genres: Sci-Fi (38%), Thriller (28%), Drama (22%)
- Stellar Run had a 72% same-day completion rate (highest ever recorded)

## Churn Analysis

### Overall Churn Rate: 4.2% monthly (down from 5.1% in Q1 2024)

### Churn Risk Factors
1. **Low engagement in first 7 days** — Viewers who watch <2 hours in their first week have 68% churn probability
2. **Genre mismatch** — Viewers who rate 3+ titles below 5/10 show 45% higher churn
3. **Price sensitivity** — Free-to-Basic conversion has 52% drop-off at payment page
4. **Content gaps** — 23% of churned viewers cited "nothing new to watch" in exit surveys

### Retention Strategies Effectiveness
| Strategy | Reduction in Churn |
|----------|-------------------|
| Personalized onboarding | -18% |
| New release notifications | -12% |
| Social features (watch parties) | -8% |
| Loyalty rewards (VIP perks) | -22% |

## Recommendations

1. **Invest in mobile-first experiences**: Optimize UI and streaming quality for mobile devices
2. **Personalized content curation**: Leverage viewing history for AI-powered recommendations
3. **Address first-week engagement**: Implement guided onboarding with personalized title suggestions
4. **Regional content expansion**: Seoul and Mumbai markets show highest growth potential
5. **Comedy genre revitalization**: Current comedy offerings don't resonate with any demographic strongly
6. **Late-night content strategy**: Develop curated late-night programming for the growing after-midnight audience
7. **Social viewing features**: Expand watch party functionality to reduce churn

## Methodology
Data collected from platform analytics, user surveys (N=12,000), and third-party market research. All viewer data has been anonymized in accordance with our data privacy policy.
