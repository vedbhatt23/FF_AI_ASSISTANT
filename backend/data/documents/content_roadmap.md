# Content Roadmap — 2025-2026

## Vision Statement

Our content strategy for 2025-2026 focuses on three pillars: **Genre Leadership** in Sci-Fi and Action, **International Expansion** with localized original content, and **Format Innovation** through interactive and short-form experiences.

## Genre Strategy

### Sci-Fi (Primary Focus)
- Maintain 30% of production budget allocation
- Expand Stellar Run into franchise (trilogy planned)
- Invest in VFX pipeline upgrades
- Target: 15M views per quarter by Q4 2025

### Action (Strong Secondary)
- 25% of production budget
- Focus on diverse action sub-genres (martial arts, espionage, military)
- Target: 12M views per quarter

### Comedy (Turnaround Strategy)
- Shift to comedy-drama hybrids
- Secure 2-3 established comedy stars for exclusive deals
- Increase comedy marketing budget by 50%
- Launch "Comedy Lab" for short-form experimental content
- Target: Reverse viewership decline by Q4 2025

### International Content
- Goal: 30% non-English content by end of 2026
- Priority markets: South Korea, India, Latin America
- Local production partnerships in 5 new markets

## Technology & Innovation
1. Interactive content engine (launching Q4 2025)
2. AI-powered personalized trailers
3. Spatial audio for Premium tier
4. 4K HDR standard for all new productions
