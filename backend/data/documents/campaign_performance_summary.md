# Marketing Campaign Performance Summary — Q1 2025

## Overview

This report summarizes the performance of all marketing campaigns executed during Q1 2025. Total marketing expenditure was $34.7 million across 87 campaigns covering 42 titles. Overall campaign ROI was 3.2x, up from 2.8x in Q4 2024.

## Channel Performance

### Social Media (38% of total spend)
- Total Spend: $13.2M
- Impressions: 892M
- Click-Through Rate: 4.2%
- Conversion Rate: 2.1%
- Top Performing Campaign: "Stellar Run Launch" — $3.4M spend, 12.8x ROI
- Social media campaigns for Sci-Fi titles outperformed all other genres by 340%

### TV Advertising (24% of total spend)
- Total Spend: $8.3M
- Estimated Reach: 45M households
- Brand Lift: +18% awareness
- Best results during prime-time sports programming slots
- Dark Orbit TV campaign drove 420K new subscriber signups

### Email Marketing (12% of total spend)
- Total Spend: $4.2M
- Open Rate: 28.4%
- Click Rate: 6.7%
- Personalized recommendation emails showed 3x higher engagement
- VIP tier members had 45% higher open rates

### Influencer Marketing (14% of total spend)
- Total Spend: $4.9M
- Total Reach: 234M
- Engagement Rate: 7.2%
- YouTube long-form reviews drove highest conversion
- TikTok short clips effective for 18-24 demographic

### Search & Display Ads (12% of total spend)
- Total Spend: $4.1M
- Search CTR: 5.8%
- Display CTR: 0.9%
- Branded search terms showing strong growth (+67% YoY)

## Campaign Highlights

### Stellar Run Launch Campaign
The integrated launch campaign for Stellar Run was our most successful campaign to date:
- Total Investment: $5.8M across all channels
- Estimated Revenue Impact: $74M
- ROI: 12.8x
- Key Success Factor: Early influencer seeding + viral TikTok moment ("StellarRunChallenge" — 2.3B views)

### Last Kingdom Heritage Campaign
Targeted campaign focusing on history enthusiasts and drama fans:
- Total Investment: $2.1M
- Estimated Revenue Impact: $12.4M
- ROI: 5.9x
- Key Success Factor: Partnership with History Channel and literary podcasts

### Comedy Genre Campaigns
Comedy campaigns significantly underperformed:
- Total Investment: $2.1M
- Estimated Revenue Impact: $3.8M
- ROI: 1.8x (below 2.0x target)
- Issue: Generic messaging, lack of viral moments, poor talent attachment

## Recommendations for Q2
1. Increase Sci-Fi and Action marketing allocation by 20%
2. Reduce traditional TV spend; reallocate to influencer and social
3. Develop comedy-specific marketing strategy with focus on talent-driven campaigns
4. Expand regional marketing teams in Mumbai and Seoul
5. Launch loyalty program integration with email campaigns
